The Best Movies According to Dae

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Matrix Trilogy

The story, fight scenes, special effects, and use of slow-motion are unparalleled.

The Fast and the Furious Saga

Great movies that portray the car underworld from the 90s to present time. Interesting character development and awaesome showcase of cars, racing scenes, and driving skills.

Resident Evil Series (Live-action and animated)

Being a fan of the old and new games, I truly enjoyed all adaptations of the series. Seeing all the famous characters, villains, and enemies portrayed outside of the game environment is pretty cool.
